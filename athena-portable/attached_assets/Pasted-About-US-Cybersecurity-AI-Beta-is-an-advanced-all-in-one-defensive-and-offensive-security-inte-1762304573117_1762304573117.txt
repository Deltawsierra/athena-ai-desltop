About US:

Cybersecurity AI Beta: is an advanced, all-in-one defensive and offensive security intelligence platform designed to detect, analyze, and prevent modern cyber threats through the power of artificial intelligence.

Built with precision and flexibility in mind, this platform integrates cutting-edge AI threat detection, penetration testing, and vulnerability classification tools into a unified system — empowering users to identify weaknesses, secure their environments, and stay ahead of evolving digital risks.



Current Features

AI-Powered Threat Detection Engine
Automatically scans and analyzes files, code, or system behavior for signs of malicious activity using advanced machine learning and transformer-based models.
CVE & Exploit Classification
Leverages natural language processing (NLP) to identify, categorize, and explain vulnerabilities by type — from SQL Injection and Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) to Authentication Bypass and Privilege Escalation.
Live Penetration Testing Module
Simulates real-world OWASP-style attacks to locate weak points in applications, APIs, and AI systems — safely and ethically, with user consent and transparency.
Admin Dashboard & Analytics
Centralized interface displaying threat summaries, scan statistics, and detection trends for fast decision-making and reporting.
PDF Reporting & Email Automation
Automatically generates detailed PDF reports for detections and penetration tests, with built-in email delivery and report logging.
Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
Secure user management system defining admin, analyst, and viewer permissions across all endpoints and dashboards.
